Caramel Apples


about this recipe:

Incredibly delicious caramel apples! The caramel coating is very gooey, so refrigerate the apples for about 15 minutes, or until the caramel has firmed up. this recipe has six servings, and takes only 25 minutes in total to cook.

what your'e going to need:

  • 6 apples
  • 6 wooden craft sticks
  • cooking spray
  • 1 (14 ounce) package individually wrapped caramels, unwrapped
  • 2 tablespoons milk

steps:

  1. Remove the stem from each apple and press a craft stick into the top. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.

  2. Place caramels and milk in a microwave-safe bowl; microwave for 2 minutes, stirring once. Set aside to cool briefly.

  3. Roll each apple quickly in caramel sauce until well coated. Place on prepared sheet to set.

and we're done! enjoy this recipe with your friends and family!
